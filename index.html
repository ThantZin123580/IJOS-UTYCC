About Us

Our Mission

We are dedicated to connecting IT students from Myanmar with the best internship opportunities across ASEAN countries. Our platform serves as a bridge between talented students and innovative companies looking for fresh perspectives and skills.

Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every student finds an internship that matches their skills, interests, and career aspirations.

Our Location

University of Technology (Yatanarpon Cyber City)

Pyin Oo Lwin, Myanmar

We are based at the prestigious University of Technology in Yatanarpon Cyber City, Myanmar's leading institution for technology education. Our central location allows us to maintain strong connections with both academic institutions and industry partners throughout Myanmar and the ASEAN region.

Visit us at our campus to learn more about our internship programs and how we can help you find the perfect opportunity to launch your IT career.

Our Team

Meet Our Dedicated Team

Our team consists of experienced professionals from the IT industry and academic sector who are passionate about helping students launch their careers. We understand the challenges faced by IT students in finding quality internships and have made it our mission to bridge this gap.

With connections to companies across ASEAN and beyond, we work to match students with opportunities that will help them grow both personally and professionally.
